About Us

Campaigner was established in 1976,44 years ago for manufacturing Industrial Laundry, Dry-Cleaning, Finishing system by employing best of hands and hardcore consultants,specialist in the field from IS,hotel and textile industry for making high quality and less maintenance equipments.

We are sister concern of one of the oldest company (LMM Engineers Combined) and have on date over 5000 installations in India,Nepal, Bhutan with India’s top Govt. Recognised Garment Export Houses, 3-5 Star Hotels, Hospitals, Laundries,Dry Cleaners etc.
